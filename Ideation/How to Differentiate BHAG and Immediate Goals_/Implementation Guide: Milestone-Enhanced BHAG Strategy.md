## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
✅ Keep the $1B BHAG visible (it's differentiating)
✅ Show current position in milestone journey
✅ Celebrate small wins along the way
✅ Be transparent about progress and setbacks
✅ Explain the systematic approach
✅ Make milestones specific and measurable

### DON'T:
❌ Hide the BHAG (it's your unique positioning)
❌ Make milestones feel like "lowering expectations"
❌ Over-complicate the milestone structure
❌ Set milestones you can't track transparently
❌ Forget to update milestone progress regularly
❌ Treat milestones as "backup plans" instead of validation stages

## Conclusion

This implementation guide provides the tactical steps to enhance your BHAG with a milestone framework. The key is to maintain the inspirational power of the billion-dollar goal while adding the credibility and trackability of systematic milestones.

**Start with the homepage hero update—it's the highest impact change you can make immediately.**

Then build out the milestone infrastructure over the next few weeks. Each addition makes the journey more tangible, trackable, and credible while maintaining the audacious vision that makes your story unique.
